How to use?

Step 1 You need to write the url of your video in the Url field.
Step 2 After writing the url, you need to enter a number in the Number field representing how many times you want the video to be played.
Step 3 After that, you have to click the Start button.
Step 4 Finally, play the video and let it run, and your views will increase.
